About Us

Makini Health is an innovative company owned and run by nurses with extensive Home Health and Visiting Nurse experience.

True to our name Makini (Swahili for attention), we give special attention to the unique needs of the Home Health Care market.

Our focus is to incorporate the end user’s experience in designing products that enhance quality of care, while promoting patient and provider safety at affordable costs for organizations and patients.
